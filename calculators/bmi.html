BMI calculator

BMI stands for "Body Mass Index". It is a numerical value used to classify individuals into different weight categories based on their height and weight. BMI provides a general indication of whether a person has a healthy body weight or is underweight, overweight, or obese.


Fit-Flame's BMI calculator tool, is designed to help you assess your body mass index and gain valuable insights into your overall health and wellness. BMI provides an indication of your weight status in relation to your height. Enter your height and weight to generate your BMI classification instantly.

BMI Calculator

After calculating your BMI, the result is compared to standard BMI ranges to determine the weight category:


  • BMI below 18.5 : Underweight
  • BMI between 18.5 and 24.9 : Normal weight
  • BMI between 25 and 29.9 : Overweight
  • BMI of 30 or above : Obese

Note: After calculating your BMI, it's important to understand the results and consider your health risks. Set goals, eat well, exercise regularly, monitor progress, seek support, and remember that BMI is a guide. Take a holistic approach to your health.


Remember, maintaining a healthy lifestyle is a continuous journey. We're here to support you along the way, so feel free to calculate your BMI anytime.
