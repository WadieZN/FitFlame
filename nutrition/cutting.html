What is "Cutting"?

Cutting, in the context of fitness and bodybuilding, refers to a phase where individuals aim to reduce body fat while preserving muscle mass. It involves creating a calorie deficit by consuming fewer calories than the body needs for maintenance, combined with a combination of cardiovascular exercise, strength training, and a balanced diet. The goal of cutting is to achieve a leaner and more defined physique by reducing body fat levels, revealing muscle definition, and achieving a lower overall body weight.

Below are 3 examples of full-day cutting diet plans that are budget-friendly:

Important : To increase cutting results, focus on these key elements:


  1. Increase Cardio: Include regular cardiovascular exercises to burn more calories and promote fat loss.

  2. High-Intensity Interval Training (HIIT): Incorporate HIIT workouts for quick and intense bursts of exercise.

  3. Maintain Strength Training: Continue strength training to preserve muscle mass and promote a toned appearance.

  4. Stay hydrated: Drink plenty of water throughout the day to support proper hydration and maintain optimal bodily functions. Water also helps with appetite control and can aid in fat loss.

  5. Consistency: Consistency is key. Stick to your cutting plan and track your progress regularly. Take measurements, monitor your weight, and take progress pictures to stay motivated and assess your results
