Deltoids

Prone Incline Shrug

What to do:

  • Maintain proper form: Lie face down on the incline bench, keeping your back straight and core engaged
  • Squeeze your shoulder blades: Focus on squeezing your shoulder blades together at the top of the movement
  • Control the weights: Use a slow and controlled motion throughout the exercise.

What to avoid:

  • Arching your back: Avoid excessive arching of the back or lifting your torso off the bench
  • Shrugging too high: Don't lift your shoulders excessively high or elevate them towards your ears

Dumbell Shrug

What to do:

  • Maintain proper posture: Stand with feet shoulder-width apart, knees slightly bent, and keep a neutral spine
  • Lift the weights vertically: Use a controlled motion to lift your shoulders directly upward toward your ears
  • Squeeze and hold: At the top of the movement, squeeze your shoulder blades together for a brief moment

What to avoid:

  • Forceful shrugging: Avoid forcefully or aggressively shrugging your shoulders
  • Rounding the back: Maintain a neutral spine throughout the exercise, avoiding rounding or arching your back

Scapula Dips

What to do:

  • Begin by hanging from a bar with your arms fully extended
  • Actively engage your muscles to pull your shoulder blades down, creating a depression
  • Maintain a strong core throughout the movement to stabilize your body

What to avoid:

  • Avoid shrugging or lifting your shoulders towards your ears
  • Prevent your shoulders from collapsing or rounding forward during the exercise
  • Do not use excessive swinging or momentum, as it reduces the effectiveness of the exercise

Dumbbell Seated Gittleson Shrug

What to do:

  • Sit upright on a bench with a dumbbell in each hand
  • Elevate your shoulders by contracting your trapezius muscles
  • Squeeze your shoulder blades together at the top of the movement

What to avoid:

  • Avoid hunching or rounding your back during the exercise
  • Do not shrug your shoulders too forcefully or aggressively, maintain controlled movements
  • Don't use excessive momentum or swinging to lift the dumbbells

Dumbell Incline Shrug

What to do:

  • Set up an incline bench at a moderate angle and sit on it with a dumbbell in each hand
  • Lift your shoulders upward in a controlled manner, focusing on contracting your upper traps
  • Squeeze your shoulder blades together at the top of the movement for maximum contraction

What to avoid:

  • Avoid arching your back or lifting your torso off the bench during the exercise
  • Don't shrug your shoulders excessively high or elevate them towards your ears
