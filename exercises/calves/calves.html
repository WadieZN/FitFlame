Calves

Lever Seated Calf Raise

What to do:

  • Sit on the machine with your feet flat on the platform
  • Position your toes and balls of your feet on the edge of the foot platform with your heels hanging off
  • Push through the balls of your feet, lifting your heels as high as possible, and squeeze your calves at the top of the movement

What to avoid:

  • Avoid rounding your back or hunching your shoulders during the exercise
  • Don't use excessive momentum or bounce to lift the platform; focus on controlled movements

Single Leg Donkey Calf Raise

What to do:

  • Stand on a raised platform or step with the ball of one foot, keeping your other foot slightly behind you
  • Push through the ball of your foot to raise your heel as high as possible, using only your calf muscles
  • Pause briefly at the top of the movement to maximize the contraction in your calf muscle before lowering your heel

What to avoid:

  • Avoid leaning too far forward or backward during the exercise
  • Don't use excessive momentum or bounce to lift your heel, focus on controlled and smooth movements

Seated Calf Press on Leg Press Machine

What to do:

  • Sit on the leg press machine with your feet on the footplate, allowing your heels to hang off the edge
  • Push through the balls of your feet to extend your ankles and raise the weight by pressing the footplate away from you
  • Squeeze your calf muscles at the top of the movement and hold for a moment before lowering the weight

What to avoid:

  • Avoid rounding your back or hunching your shoulders during the exercise
  • Don't use excessive weight that compromises your form

Resistance Band Foot External Rotation

What to do:

  • Sit on a chair or bench with your feet flat on the floor and a resistance band looped around one foot
  • Keeping your heel planted, rotate your foot outward against the resistance of the band.
  • Hold the outward position for a moment, feeling the contraction in the muscles on the outer side of your foot, then return to the starting position

What to avoid:

  • Avoid allowing your heel to lift off the floor during the exercise, keep it firmly planted
  • Do not overly tense or strain your foot muscles

Barbell Seated Calf Raise

What to do:

  • Sit on a calf raise machine or bench with your thighs secured under the pads and a barbell placed on top of your knee
  • Place the balls of your feet on an elevated platform, with your heels hanging off the edge
  • Push through the balls of your feet to raise your heels as high as possible. lower them back down

What to avoid:

  • Avoid rounding your back or hunching your shoulders during the exercise
  • Do not lock your knees at the top of the movement
