Chest

Push-ups

What to do:

  • Start in a high plank position with hands slightly wider than shoulder-width apart
  • Lower your body by bending your elbows until your chest is just above the ground
  • Push back up by straightening your arms, maintaining a straight body position

What to avoid:

  • Sagging or raising your hips too high
  • Don't let your elbows flare out to the sides
  • Rushing the movement, maintain controlled motions

Bench Press

What to do:

  • Lie on a flat bench with your feet firmly planted on the ground and a barbell gripped slightly wider than shoulder-width apart
  • Lower the barbell to your chest by bending your elbows, keeping them at a 90-degree angle
  • Push the barbell back up by extending your arms, keeping your wrists straight and maintaining a stable grip

What to avoid:

  • Avoid arching your back excessively, keep your lower back pressed against the bench
  • Don't bounce the barbell off your chest, maintain control throughout the movement

Lever Chest Press

What to do:

  • Sit on the lever chest press machine with your feet flat on the floor and your back pressed to the backrest
  • Grab the handles or grips and push them forward, extending your arms in front of you
  • Squeeze your chest muscles at the end of the movement and hold for a brief moment before slowly releasing the handles back to the starting position

What to avoid:

  • Avoid rounding your back or hunching your shoulders during the exercise
  • Don't use excessive momentum or jerking motions

Pec Deck Fly

What to do:

  • Sit on the pec deck machine with your back pressed against the backrest and grasp the handles
  • Keep your elbows slightly bent and your arms parallel to the floor
  • Squeeze your chest muscles as you bring the handles together in front of your chest, then slowly return to the starting position

What to avoid:

  • Avoid rounding your back or hunching your shoulders during the exercise
  • Don't use excessive weight that compromises your form

Dumbbell Fly

What to do:

  • Lie flat on a bench with a dumbbell in each hand, palms facing inward
  • Extend your arms out to the sides with a slight bend in your elbows
  • Lower the dumbbells in a controlled manner, keeping a slight bend in your elbows, until you feel a stretch in your chest. Then, bring the dumbbells back up to the starting position by squeezing your chest muscles

What to avoid:

  • Avoid using excessive weight that compromises your form or puts excessive strain on your shoulders or chest
  • Don't lower the dumbbells too far down or allow your shoulders to excessively rotate

Narrow Grip Wall Push-Up

What to do:

  • Stand facing a wall and place your hands on the wall slightly narrower than shoulder-width apart
  • Step back to create a slight angle with your body, keeping your feet hip-width apart
  • Lower your chest toward the wall by bending your elbows, keeping them close to your sides, and then push back to the starting position

What to avoid:

  • Avoid arching or rounding your back during the exercise
  • Don't allow your elbows to flare out to the sides
